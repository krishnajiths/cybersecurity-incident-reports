# Canvas Ransomware Attack

## Date
May 7, 2026

## Target
Canvas users and universities

## Sector
Education Sector

## Type of Attack
Ransomware

## Threat Actor
ShinyHunters

## Attack Method
Hackers allegedly breached Canvas systems and deployed ransomware. The exact attack method has not been disclosed.

## Impact
Thousands of students and multiple universities were affected during finals week.

## Data Compromised
Names, email addresses, student ID numbers, and messages.

## Response Taken
Privileges were revoked, malware was contained, security patches were deployed, and monitoring was increased.

## Current Status
Solved

## Solution
Canvas reportedly paid the ransom and restored services while strengthening platform security.

## Short Summary
The ransomware attack temporarily disrupted Canvas services during finals week. Attackers threatened to leak sensitive user data if the ransom was not paid.

## Lesson Learned
Educational organizations should prepare backup systems and avoid relying entirely on online platforms during critical periods.

## Severity Rating
High
